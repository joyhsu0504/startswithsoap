Mission

Our timeline to scale this initiative.

  • April 2016

    Where it all started

    This past weekend, the 2016 Coca Cola Scholars had the opportunity to volunteer at an elementary school in an underprivileged area of Atlanta, and we saw that some of the bathrooms didn’t even have soap dispensers for the students to use. Unfortunately, this is one of several similar situations around the country that kids are facing everyday. It got us thinking: what could we do to give these schools a hand?

Parklane Elementary

Hands on Atlanta project.

Starts with Soap is a fundraising initiative aiming to personally reach out to schools with a need for basic supplies, currently represented nationwide by accomplished student leaders. Right now, our goal is to raise as much awareness and as many funds for the cause as we can across the country, for the school we visited and countless others in our areas. This is where you come in. Any support we receive from you, whether by donation or social media sharing, makes Starts with Soap an accessible resource for schools across America. Follow our campaign and join the movement as we Start with Soap.
